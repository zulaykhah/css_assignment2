THIRD SECTION

Differences between Paddings, Margins, and borders

Paddings: The inner space between the content and the border of your box.
Margins: Is the outer space (or lack of space) surrounding the box.
Borders: The perimeter of the box. Borders can be invisible or they could be a thick colored line.
